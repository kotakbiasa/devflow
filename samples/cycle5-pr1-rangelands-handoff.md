## Risks & landmines
- **Language selector is display-only.** The UI shows "English" with a chevron, but there is no dropdown behavior implemented. If users click it, nothing happens. This could be perceived as a broken feature.
- **CMS dependency.** The Stelarr and StoriesCards sections fetch content from the CMS. If the expected slugs or images are missing, these sections will render empty or with missing visuals.
- **Complex responsive geometry.** The Livestock section uses rotated squares and percentage-based positioning. These shapes may shift unexpectedly on uncommon viewport sizes.
- **Scroll performance.** Multiple `useInView` observers and motion animations are active. On low-end devices, this could cause jank during fast scrolling.
- **Image asset assumptions.** The code references specific image paths (e.g., `/images/home/home-of-many.png`, pattern files). If assets are missing or renamed, sections will show broken images.

1. **Section order:** Load the home page and confirm the seven sections appear in the order listed above.

4. **StoriesCards:** Confirm two cards appear with titles, images, and captions. Click a card to navigate to the story; click the "See all" link to navigate to the filtered stories page.
5. **Resources animation:** Scroll to the resources section and confirm the three cards fade in and slide up when they enter the viewport.
6. **Responsive check:** Test at mobile (375px), tablet (768px), and desktop (1440px) breakpoints. Verify the geometric shapes in Livestock and the card layouts adapt cleanly.
7. **CMS fallback:** Temporarily disable the CMS query in Stelarr or StoriesCards and confirm the sections handle missing data gracefully (no runtime errors).
